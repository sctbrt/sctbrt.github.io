Systems that make things feel lighter.

Reducing friction in how teams plan, decide, and hand off — especially when things are busy, imperfect, or changing. The goal isn't perfection. It's clearer decisions, fewer unforced errors, and more room to focus on what actually matters.

Most days feel heavier than they need to be. This is an attempt to change that.

Most things don't fail because people aren't trying hard enough.

They fail quietly — through unclear ownership, fragile handoffs, and decisions that never quite settle. Over time, that friction adds weight to the day.

This practice exists to remove some of that weight.
